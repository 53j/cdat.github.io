Therefore, it is necessary to specify the type of font needed for a particular plot. This section will textually show the 9 different fonts. Lines can be used to draw from one point to another or to outline information. This section will visually show the 5 different line types. Markers can be used to identify points of data. This section will visually show the 17 different marker types. Patterns can be used to fill in areas between data.
